About UQ MARS

Discover who we are, what we do, and how we foster a thriving robotics community at UQ.

Who We Are

UQ MARS (Mechatronics and Robotics Society) is a student-driven organization founded as UQ Robotics and rebranded in 2019. Our mission is to inspire and support students in robotics, from academic workshops to competitive challenges and industry connections. We are committed to helping students explore their passion for robotics and technology.

Our activities include hands-on technical workshops, networking events, and team-based projects that allow students to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world challenges. Whether you're just starting out or a seasoned enthusiast, UQ MARS is your hub for robotics at UQ.

Student Driven

UQ MARS is all about creating a collaborative environment for students to learn, grow, and connect. Our events, including workshops, games nights, and social gatherings, are designed to build strong relationships among members and foster a sense of community.

We also participate in global robotics competitions, where students can take projects from concept to execution. These competitions help our members develop technical skills, teamwork, and confidence as future engineers.

Meet Our Executive Team

UQ MARS is run by a dedicated team of undergraduate students who share a passion for robotics and technology. They work tirelessly to organize events, manage projects, and support our members. Get to know the people behind the society who make it all happen.
