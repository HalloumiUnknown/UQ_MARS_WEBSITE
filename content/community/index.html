Discover how UQ MARS can enhance your university experience by providing opportunities to build technical expertise, develop lifelong friendships, and gain valuable insights into the world of robotics and mechatronics.

Events

At UQ MARS, we host a diverse range of events to inspire and engage our community. Our signature hackathons challenge members to think creatively and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems in a high-energy, collaborative environment. We also organize industry talks and seminars, where professionals share insights into cutting-edge technologies, career advice, and advancements in robotics and mechatronics. For a more immersive experience, our industry site visits provide a behind-the-scenes look at leading companies and research facilities, giving members an opportunity to learn directly from experts in the field. Additionally, our workshops focus on building essential technical skills, while social events like trivia nights and games evenings offer a fun and relaxed space for students to connect. Each event is thoughtfully designed to foster collaboration, spark innovation, and strengthen the bond within our thriving robotics community.
