Events

UQ MARS (Mechatronics and Robotics Society) is a student-driven organization founded as UQ Robotics and rebranded in 2019. Our mission is to inspire and support students in robotics, from academic workshops to competitive challenges and industry connections. We are committed to helping students explore their passion for robotics and technology.
